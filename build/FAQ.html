Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our condominium association management services.

What types of properties do you manage?
We specialize exclusively in managing condominium associations in the Chicago area, providing tailored services to meet their unique governance and operational needs.
How does T.H.E. Management handle association finances?
We provide comprehensive financial services including paying all association bills, managing accounts receivable and tracking delinquent owners, assisting with bank account setup, and generating detailed monthly revenue/expenditure statements and progress reports for the Board.
Can you help associations that are struggling or "distressed"?
Absolutely. We specialize in supporting distressed associations of any size. We can help address challenges like significant arrears, low reserves, maintenance issues, code violations, and provide guidance and training to get your association back on track.
Do you offer emergency services?
Yes, ensuring the safety and smooth operation of your property is crucial. We provide 24/7 emergency response services for urgent issues that require immediate attention.
What area do you serve?
Our primary service area is the greater Chicago metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs within Illinois. Please contact us to discuss your specific location.
How do we get started?
The best way to start is by contacting us via our contact form or phone number. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your association's specific needs and how our tailored services can help.
